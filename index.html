Good to know

Frequently asked

Is this an official World Cup app?
No. Matchday 26 is an independent, unofficial fan app. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with FIFA, the FIFA World Cup™, or any official organising body. Match data comes from publicly available feeds, and all trademarks belong to their respective owners.
How much does it cost?
The app and all of its core features — live scores, schedule, groups, bracket, reminders and the widget — are free. Optional national-team themes are available as small one-time in-app purchases, and the clean default theme is free forever.
What data does it collect?
None. There are no ads, no analytics, no trackers and no account. Your settings stay on your device, match data is read from a public feed, and any purchases are handled by Google Play. See the privacy policy for the details.
Will I get notified when my team plays?
Yes. Enable notifications and you'll get a reminder 15 minutes before kickoff, plus a live-score notification that updates through the match — even with the app closed.
Which devices are supported?
Android phones and tablets running Android 8.0 or newer.
