How it works

From "where can I study?" to a seat, in three taps

Open the app

It instantly lists every study hall and library, marking what's open now and how many seats are free.

Pick the closest open spot

Sort by free seats or distance, check the map and today's hours, and head over knowing there's room.

Star it for next time

Favourite the rooms you use and let Seients ping you when they free up — so your spot finds you.

Honest data

Straight from the city, clearly labelled

Availability comes from the Ajuntament de Barcelona's open data and the city's study-hall service. Seients relays it faithfully and tells you exactly how fresh each reading is — counts only refresh while a room is open, and it says so plainly instead of pretending.
