Privacy Policy

Effective date: 6 June 2026 · Last updated: 6 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Matchday 26 mobile application (“the App”) handles information. The App is developed and published independently (“we”, “us”, “our”).

Contact: cafonsomota@gmail.com

The short version. Matchday 26 does not collect, store, or share your personal information. There is no account to create, no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party tracking. Everything you set in the App stays on your device.

Information we do not collect

We do not collect, request, or receive any of the following:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any account/sign-in information (the App has no accounts)
  • Your location
  • Contacts, photos, files, or other personal content
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Analytics or usage/behaviour data
  • Crash logs or diagnostics sent to us

We run no servers of our own and have no backend that could receive your data.

Information stored on your device

To make the App work, your choices are saved locally on your device only, using the operating system's standard storage. This includes:

  • Your selected and followed team(s)
  • Your chosen theme, language, and dark-mode preference
  • Notification and match-reminder settings
  • Which optional themes you have unlocked (purchase entitlements)

This information never leaves your device, is never transmitted to us, and is removed when you uninstall the App.

Network requests (match data)

To show fixtures, scores, and standings, the App requests publicly available match data from a public football data feed (api.fifa.com). Like any internet request, these requests necessarily include your device's IP address and standard technical metadata, which are processed by those third-party servers and their content-delivery networks under their own privacy policies. We do not receive, see, or store this information.

Purchases

Optional in-app purchases (team themes) are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We never see or store your payment-card details. Google provides us only with the minimal, anonymous purchase information needed to unlock the content you bought. Your purchase is handled under Google's Privacy Policy.

Notifications

With your permission, the App displays match reminders and live-score notifications. These are generated on your device from match data; no notification content is sent to or collected by us. You can disable notifications at any time in the App or in your device settings.

Permissions we request

  • Internet / network state — to fetch match data.
  • Notifications (Android 13+) — to show kickoff reminders and live scores. Optional.
  • Foreground service — to keep the live-score notification updated during a match. Used only while a match you follow is live.

Children's privacy

The App is suitable for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. Because we collect no personal data at all, none is collected from children.

Third-party services

  • Public football data feed (api.fifa.com) — source of fixtures, scores, and standings.
  • Google Play Billing — processes optional in-app purchases. See Google's Privacy Policy.

We are not affiliated with these providers, and their handling of any data is governed by their own policies.

Data security

Because we do not collect or transmit your personal data to any server we control, there is no personal data of yours for us to hold or to lose. Information stored on your device is protected by your device's operating system and security features.

Your rights

As we hold no personal data about you, there is nothing on our side to access, correct, export, or delete. Uninstalling the App removes all of its on-device data. For questions about purchase records, please contact Google Play.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above reflects the latest version, and material changes will be reflected here and, where appropriate, in the App or store listing.

Contact
